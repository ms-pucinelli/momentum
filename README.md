# Momentum

Momentum is a gamified habit tracker and emotional journal that helps users build consistency through small daily actions, weekly goals, and positive reflections.

The app was created around a simple idea: you don't need perfect days to make progress. Every small action contributes to your momentum.

✨ Build habits.

⭐ Earn points.

📈 Track your progress.

📖 Remember your good days.

💛 Keep moving forward.
